# ✅ How to Reuse the Same Vehicle for Multiple Bookings

## The System Already Supports This!

A car owner can use the same vehicle for unlimited bookings. Each booking is independent.

## How It Works

### Scenario:
1. **Day 1:** Car owner books "Toyota Corolla" for parking service → Service completed
2. **Day 7:** Car owner needs maintenance for the same "Toyota Corolla" → Can book again!
3. **Day 14:** Car owner needs repair for the same "Toyota Corolla" → Can book again!

### The Process:

#### Step 1: Add Your Vehicle Once
- Go to "My Vehicles"
- Add your vehicle (Toyota Corolla, Plate: AA-12345)
- This vehicle stays in your account forever

#### Step 2: Make First Booking
- Go to "Find Garage"
- Click "Reserve Now"
- Select your vehicle: "Toyota Corolla"
- Choose service type: "Parking"
- Submit booking
- Service gets completed

#### Step 3: Make Second Booking (Same Vehicle)
- Go to "Find Garage" again
- Click "Reserve Now" on any garage
- Select the SAME vehicle: "Toyota Corolla"
- Choose different service: "Maintenance"
- Submit booking
- ✅ Works perfectly!

#### Step 4: Make Third Booking (Same Vehicle)
- Repeat the process
- Same vehicle can be used unlimited times
- Each booking is separate

## Key Points

1. **One Vehicle, Many Bookings**
   - You add a vehicle once
   - Use it for unlimited bookings
   - No need to add the vehicle again

2. **Booking Status Doesn't Block Vehicle**
   - Completed booking? ✅ Vehicle available for new booking
   - Cancelled booking? ✅ Vehicle available for new booking
   - Active booking? ⚠️ Vehicle can still be booked (different time slot)

3. **No Restrictions**
   - Same vehicle can have multiple bookings
   - Different garages
   - Different service types
   - Different dates/times

## How to View Your Booking History

1. Go to "My Reservations"
2. See all bookings for all your vehicles
3. Filter by status:
   - Pending (waiting for garage confirmation)
   - Confirmed (garage accepted)
   - Completed (service finished)
   - Cancelled (booking cancelled)

You can add multiple vehicles and use any of them:

**Your Vehicles:**
- Toyota Corolla (AA-12345)

**When booking:**
- Choose which vehicle needs service
