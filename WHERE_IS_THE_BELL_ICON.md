## If You Don't See It

### Check 1: Are You Logged In?
Look for your name in the top-right corner:
- ✅ If you see: `[🔔] [👤 kene] [Logout]` → You're logged in
- ❌ If you see: `[Login] [Get Started]` → You need to login

### Check 2: Refresh the Page
Press **F5** or **Ctrl+R**

### Check 3: Check the Navbar
The bell is ONLY in the navbar (the dark bar at the very top).
It's NOT in the sidebar (the left menu).

## Colors to Look For

- **Navbar background:** Dark blue/black
- **Bell icon:** White/light color
- **Badge:** Red circle with white number
- **Your name:** White text
- **Logout button:** Red text

## Still Can't Find It?

1. **Take a screenshot** of your entire browser window
2. **Check if you're on the right page** (not login/register page)
3. **Make sure you're logged in** (see your name in top-right)
4. **Try a different browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
5. **Clear browser cache** (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
