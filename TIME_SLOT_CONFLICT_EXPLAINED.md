# ✅ Time Slot Conflict - This is Normal!

## What Happened

You got this error:
```
"This time slot is already reserved for this garage"
```

**This is CORRECT behavior!** The system is protecting you from double-booking.

## Why This Happens

The garage cannot have two bookings at the same time. For example:

**Conflict Example:**
```
Booking 1: Jan 10, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM at Jimma Central
Booking 2: Jan 10, 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM at Jimma Central ❌ CONFLICT!
```

The times overlap, so the second booking is rejected.

## How to Book Again with Same Vehicle

You have 3 options:

### Option 1: Choose Different Time
**Same garage, same day, different time:**
```
✅ Booking 1: Jan 10, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
✅ Booking 2: Jan 10, 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM (No overlap!)
```

### Option 2: Choose Different Date
**Same garage, different day:**
```
✅ Booking 1: Jan 10, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
✅ Booking 2: Jan 15, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM (Different day!)
```

### Option 3: Choose Different Garage
**Same time, different garage:**
```
✅ Booking 1: Jan 10, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM at Jimma Central
✅ Booking 2: Jan 10, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM at Jimma Hassen (Different garage!)
```

## Your Vehicle is NOT the Problem

- ✅ Same vehicle can have unlimited bookings
- ✅ The issue is the TIME SLOT at that specific garage
- ✅ Just change the time or date

## Step-by-Step: Make a New Booking

1. **Go to "Find Garage"**
2. **Click "Reserve Now"**
3. **Select your vehicle** (same one is fine!)
4. **Choose DIFFERENT time:**
   - If previous booking was 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
   - Try 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
   - Or choose tomorrow's date
5. **Submit** - Should work now!

## Real-World Example

**Scenario:** You have a Toyota Corolla

**Monday:**
- 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Parking at Jimma Central ✅ Success
- 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Maintenance at Jimma Central ✅ Success (different time)
- 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Parking at Jimma Hassen ✅ Success (different garage)

**Tuesday:**
- 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Repair at Jimma Central ✅ Success (different day)

All bookings use the SAME vehicle!

## How to Check Existing Bookings

1. Go to "My Reservations"
2. See all your active bookings
3. Check the times to avoid conflicts
4. Plan your next booking accordingly

## Summary

- ❌ **Error:** "Time slot already reserved" = You tried to book an occupied time
- ✅ **Solution:** Change the time, date, or garage
- ✅ **Your vehicle:** Can be used unlimited times
- ✅ **The system:** Prevents double-booking (this is good!)

---

**Just pick a different time slot and try again!**
