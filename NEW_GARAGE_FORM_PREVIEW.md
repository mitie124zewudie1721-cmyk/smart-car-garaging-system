## How It Works

### Adding Multiple Services
1. Form starts with 1 service field
2. Click "+ Add Another Service" to add more
3. Each service has its own:
   - Name field
   - Price field
   - Duration field
   - Description field
4. Click "Remove" to delete a service
5. Must have at least 1 service

### Bank Account Details
1. Initially hidden to keep form clean
2. Click "+ Add Bank Account Details" to show
3. Fill in only the banks you use
4. All fields are optional
5. Click "− Hide Bank Account Details" to collapse

### Validation
- Required fields marked with *
- Real-time validation as you type
- Error messages appear below fields
- Can't submit until all required fields are valid
- At least 1 service required
- At least 1 payment method required

## What Happens After Submit

1. **Form Validation**
   - All required fields checked
   - Format validation (email, phone, time)
   - At least 1 service required
   - At least 1 payment method required

2. **Data Transformation**
   - Form data converted to backend format
   - Coordinates formatted as [lng, lat]
   - Services array structured correctly
   - Bank accounts organized by type

3. **API Call**
   - POST to `/api/garages`
   - Includes all form data
   - Sets verificationStatus to "pending"

4. **Success Response**
   - Success toast message
   - Redirect to "My Garages" page
   - Garage appears with "Pending" status

5. **Admin Review**
   - Admin sees garage in verification queue
   - Admin can approve or reject
   - Notification sent to garage owner

## Troubleshooting

### Form doesn't load
- Check browser console for errors
- Hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R
- Clear cache and reload

### Can't submit form
- Check for validation errors (red text)
- Ensure all required fields filled
- At least 1 service added
- At least 1 payment method selected

### Data not saving
- Check MongoDB connection (see FIX_MONGODB_CONNECTION.md)
- Check backend console for errors
- Verify backend is running

### Services not appearing
- Check that you clicked "+ Add Another Service"
- Ensure each service has name, price, duration
- Check for validation errors
