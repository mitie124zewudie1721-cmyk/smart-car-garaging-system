# ✅ View Details Modal is Now Working!

## What Was Added

The "View Details" button now opens a full modal showing ALL garage information:

- Garage name and address
- Price per hour
- Available slots
- Rating and reviews
- Services offered
- Amenities (WiFi, CCTV, etc.)
- Operating hours
- Contact information
- Photos (if available)
- **Reserve Now button inside the modal**

## How It Works

1. **Click "View Details"** on any garage card
2. **Modal opens** showing complete garage information
3. **Review all details** - services, pricing, amenities, ratings
4. **Click "Reserve Now"** inside the modal to book
5. **Reservation form opens** with that garage pre-selected

## User Flow

```
Garage Card
    ↓
[View Details] ← Click this
    ↓
Modal with Full Info
    ↓
[Reserve Now] ← Click this after reviewing
    ↓
Reservation Form
```

## Both Buttons Still Work

- **View Details** (outline) → Opens modal with full garage info
- **Reserve Now** (primary) → Directly opens reservation form

## Next Step

**Refresh your browser** with `Ctrl + Shift + R` to see the modal working!

The modal will show all garage details and allow the car owner to make an informed decision before reserving.
